### Q: Waiting for the parts to get in so I can do this. Question tho, how did you put the headlight housing back together? Did you back them again to soften the material it has and put the screw back in? Or did you use new glue material and reapplied it? Or no glue at all?
*Asked by @joseostorva7235 (2 likes)*

**A:** So I've done this a handful of times now, and I've never had a moisture/condensation issue.

MOST of the time, I just reuse the butyl sealant that was on the headlight. Once the build is done, I put the lens back on as tight as I can by hand, then put it back in the oven to warm up the sealant. I pull it out and immediately screw everything back tight.

I've replaced the butyl sealant once. Not necessary in my opinion unless you're extra paranoid. Be sure to remove all the old stuff before putting new stuff down, or it gets messy.
